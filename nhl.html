NHL Goalie Chart

Inspired by graphics at the New York Times and Deadspin, this chart displays the number of games each goalie played for all NHL teams from 2000-2013. Goalies are only included if they played more than 5 games for that team. The teams are ordered by the number of different goalies who have played for them during this period. Note that each team has not played the same number of games during this time period, since the data include playoff games. Data from Hockey Reference.
