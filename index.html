About

I love working with data. I went to school in Portland, Oregon at Reed College and graduated in 2014 with a BA in Mathematics-Economics. At Reed I studied Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, econometric methods, Probability Theory and Statistics, and Computing Theory. I wrote my thesis on the efficiency of sports betting markets. In the fall of 2013, I started a blog writing about an analytic approach to soccer. I've since been featured on Stats Bomb. You can find me in Portland or on Twitter .
